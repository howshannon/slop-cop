# Credits & prior art

Slop Cop didn't invent the idea of hunting AI slop. It synthesizes and de-conflicts several open-source projects and a body of recent research into one routed skill with a forced scoring procedure.

## Source projects

- **stop-slop** — Hardik Pandya
- **slopless** — seochecks.ai
- **hallmark** — Nutlope
- **taste-skill** — Leonxlnx
- **desloppify** — Peter O'Malley
- **Rossmann Group no-AI-slop writing rules**

## Research the beats were hardened with

- **Prose** — updated for the 2026 landscape, where the em dash is a fading tell and cadence uniformity is the strongest signal.
- **Design** — generated-UI research, including the roughly 50ms credibility judgment and the indigo-gradient / Inter default cluster.
- **Code** — LLM code-smell findings: happy-path scope, over-documentation of the obvious, and higher smell density on more complex tasks.

## What Slop Cop adds

A single routed entry point across prose, design, and code; a false-positive-prevention layer so it tickets slop and not vocabulary; and a forced scoring procedure with calibration anchors, so grades stay consistent instead of drifting on gut feel.
